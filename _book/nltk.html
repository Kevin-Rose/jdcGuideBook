NLTK

Manual installation

Create a foldernltk_data, e.g.C:\nltk_data, or/usr/local/share/nltk_data, and subfolderschunkers,grammars,misc,sentiment,taggers,corpora,help,models,stemmers,tokenizers.

Download individual packages fromhttp://nltk.org/nltk_data/(see the “download” links). Unzip them to the appropriate subfolder. For example, the Brown Corpus, found at:https://raw.githubusercontent.com/nltk/nltk_data/gh-pages/packages/corpora/brown.zipis to be unzipped tonltk_data/corpora/brown.

Set yourNLTK_DATAenvironment variable to point to your top levelnltk_datafolder.
